Sesame Noodles

Description

Simple, quick and easy recipe for cold sesame noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 package fresh egg noodles
  • ¼ cup sesame seeds
  • ¼ cup tamari
  • ¼ cup sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons Shaosing cooking wine
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, or sucanat
  • ¼ teasoon chili paste
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions(garnish)

    • Steps

    1. Boil noodles for 30 seconds or less, drain
    2. Heat oil, sesame seeds, chili paste in pan on medium heat
    3. Whisk tamari, sesame paste, cooking wine and sugar
    4. Turn off heat. Add noodles to hot oil. Then whisked sesame sauce and mix
    5. Garnish with green onions
