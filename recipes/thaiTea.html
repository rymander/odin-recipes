Thai Tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup thai tea
  • 4 cups water
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup half & half, or sweetened condensed milk(omit extra sugar)

    • Steps

      Bring water to boil, add thai tea mix. Add sugar and mix. Gently boil for 3 minutes. Remove from heat
      Steep 30 minutes, allow to cool
      Strain using a cloth filter
      Fill glass with ice, pour strained tea leaving room for cream. Enjoy
